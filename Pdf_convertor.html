How to Use the Document Converter

1. Upload Your Document

Drag and drop your file into the upload area or click the "Choose File" button. We support PDF, DOCX, and TXT formats.

2. Select Target Format

Choose the format you want to convert your document to from the dropdown menu.

3. Convert

Click the "Convert Document" button and wait for the conversion process to complete.

4. Download

Once conversion is complete, click the "Download" button to save your converted document.

Conversion Support

This tool supports the following conversion paths:

  • PDF to DOCX or TXT
  • DOCX to PDF or TXT
  • TXT to PDF or DOCX
