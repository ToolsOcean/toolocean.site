Base64 Encoder/Decoder

Convert text to Base64 or decode Base64 back to plain text

How to Use

To Encode Text to Base64:

1. Enter or paste your plain text in the input field

2. Click the "Encode to Base64" button

3. Copy the resulting Base64 string from the output field

To Decode Base64 to Text:

1. Enter or paste Base64 encoded text in the input field

2. Click the "Decode from Base64" button

3. View the decoded plain text in the output field
